QTc Calculator

Enter the QT interval and RR interval in milliseconds to calculate corrected QT intervals using multiple formulas.

Formulas Used:

  • Bazett's formula: QTc = QT / √(RR in seconds)
  • Fridericia's formula: QTc = QT / (RR in seconds)1/3
  • Framingham's formula: QTc = QT + 0.154 × (1,000 - RR)
  • Hodges' formula: QTc = QT + 1.75 × (60 / (RR in seconds) - 60)
